# Deep Research

Use this skill for broad investigations, competing claims, or requests that require multi-hop synthesis.

## Workflow

1. Clarify the research target, constraints, and success criteria from the prompt and repo context.
2. Break the topic into a small set of research threads.
3. Gather sources methodically, preferring primary sources when available.
4. Track confidence, contradictions, and missing evidence as you go.
5. Synthesize findings into a direct answer with explicit uncertainty.

## Output

Include:
- the answer or current best conclusion
- the strongest supporting evidence
- unresolved gaps or conflicts
- the next investigation step if the question remains open

## Guardrails

- Follow evidence, not narrative momentum.
- Do not hide uncertainty when sources conflict or are weak.
